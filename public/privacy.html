Overview

VenusResource is operated by Corgan Studio ("we," "us," "our"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Venus spatial intelligence platform at venusresource.net.

We collect only what's necessary to provide the service. VenusResource is a visualization platform, not a social network.

Information We Collect

Account information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password via Supabase.

Usage data: Anonymous usage statistics only. No cross-site tracking.

Payment information: Processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card details.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and database
  • Stripe: Subscription payments
  • Cloudflare: Content delivery and DDoS protection
  • NASA / JPL / ESA: Scientific data sources

Data Security

All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS. Authentication tokens are handled by Supabase with industry-standard practices.

Cookies

Minimal cookies for authentication session management only. No advertising or tracking cookies.
