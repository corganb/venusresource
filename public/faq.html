What is VenusResource?
VenusResource is the Corgan Studio Venus intelligence platform, part of the Resource Systems multi-planetary suite. Venus spatial intelligence platform - Magellan radar, VIRTIS atmosphere, Akatsuki imaging, VEx archive.
What is Resource Systems?
Resource Systems is the first multi-planetary spatial intelligence platform, bringing together Earth, Moon, Mars, the Sun, and all major bodies in the Solar System into a unified suite with live data, mission tracking, and cross-body analytics. Created by Jason Corgan Brown at Corgan Studio. Launched 2025.
What's currently live on VenusResource?
v0.1 scaffold: Three.js 3D scene of Venus, shared authentication, Deep Space Network live panel, cross-site navigation to the other Resource Systems sibling sites. Real terrain data, mission tracking, and live layers arrive in v0.2.
Who created this?
Jason Corgan Brown, founder of Corgan Studio. Jason's work spans spatial design for projects including NEOM, Amazon Studios, and the Obama Presidential Center, alongside creative leadership on Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, and Tron. Resource Systems launched in 2025.
